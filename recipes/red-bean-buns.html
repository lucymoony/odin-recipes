Red Bean Buns

Description

Homemade Chinese red bean buns with a soft and slightly sweet surface.

Ingredients

Red bean paste

  • ½ cup red beans (pre-soaked)
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 400 ml water
  • 1 tbsp. butter or vegetable oil. (optional)

Dough

  • 300 g all purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1.5 tsp instant yeast
  • 150 ml cold water or 180 ml milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

Red bean paste

  1. Add water, sugar, and soaked red beans into a pot and cook. Transfer to a non-stick pan, and melt in butter (optional, you can use vegetable cooking oil too).
    Stir fry until the paste can stick together.

Dough

  1. Add yeast and sugar in cold water or milk. Mix well.
  2. Add flour and yeast water to a stand mixer and mix for around 1 minute at low speed.
    Add vegetable oil (optional) and continue kneading for at least 10 minutes until the dough is super smooth and elastic.
  3. Take the dough out, slightly dust the surface you will use to roll out the dough, and then divide the dough into 8 portions.
  4. Take one portion out. Knead the dough for a couple of minutes, and then shape it into a smooth ball.
  5. Press down and then roll out slightly to a flat circle, then turn it over (so the smoother side is on the outside) and roll to get a thick bottom.

Assemble

  1. Place some of the red bean paste in the center, gather the dough slowly using the space between your thumb and forefinger, and then tear off the excess dough.
  2. Turn the bun over and shape it into a dome (a little bit higher than you want because it will flatten a little bit ). Repeat to finish the remaining buns.
  3. Line your steamer and place the buns one by one, with space among each other.
  4. Cover the lid and now let them start rising. The time depends on lots of factors, including the room temperature and then the moisture.
    All we need to do is to watch the size of the dough. Your buns will be ready to steam when they are 1.5 times in size.
  5. When the buns are ready to steam, put the steamer in a pan with cold water and steam for around 13 minutes (on medium to low heat after the water starts to boil).
    Then remove from heat and let it stand for 5 minutes.
